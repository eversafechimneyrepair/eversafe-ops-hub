💵 Deposit & Milestone Structure

To schedule your project and secure the necessary raw materials, EverSafe Chimney Repair utilizes the following standard deposit framework:

  • Standard Repair Projects (Under $1,500): A 10% scheduling deposit is required to lock in your project date, with the remaining balance due immediately upon completion.
  • Major Structural Projects (Over $1,500): A 50% material mobilization deposit is required prior to scheduling. The remaining 50% balance is due immediately upon on-site project completion.
  • Custom Material Orders: Any non-standard custom fabricated chase tops, liners, or specialized masonry elements require a 100% upfront material payment. This deposit is non-refundable once custom manufacturing begins.

🛡️ Warranty Policy

We take pride in our craftsmanship. EverSafe Chimney Repair provides the following specific warranties upon project closeout and full payment settlement:

  • Mortar & Brickwork: Includes a 2-year craftsmanship warranty covering structural soundness, joint integrity, and repointing stability under normal environmental exposure. (Does not cover damage caused by settling foundations, lightning strikes, or pre-existing internal structural shifting).
  • Continuous Relining Systems: Includes a lifetime material warranty against structural decay, provided the flue is professionally swept and inspected annually.
  • Cement Crown Pours: Warranted for 5 years against deep structural cracking or water penetration. (Does not cover minor superficial hairline curing checks).
  • Flashing Elements: Warranted for 3 years against wind-driven leaks or material separation under standard weather conditions.
