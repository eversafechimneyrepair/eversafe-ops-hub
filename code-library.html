☔ Leak Diagnostic Matrix
Diagnostic Profile
Rain Leak vs. Mechanical Condensation Rain: Occurs during/after storms. Brown, sooty water. Condensation: Occurs in freezing weather. Highly acidic, clear/white chalky water running out of cleanouts.
📖 Read the "Why"

The Fix: If the chimney leaks during a sunny, 20-degree winter week, the flashing didn't fail. A high-efficiency gas furnace or water heater is condensing inside the cold masonry flue. Selling a crown rebuild won't fix it. You must install a proper AL29-4C insulated liner to contain the mechanical condensation.
