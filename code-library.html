🚫 Defect Limits & Failure Criteria
NFPA 211 - 14.4.1
Creosote Buildup Sweep if > 1/8" thick Fails if Stage 3 (Glaze) is present
📖 Read the "Why"
Stage 3 looks like shiny, hard, black glass. It expands up to 7 times its size when ignited, blocking the flue and pushing a 2000-degree blowtorch into the living room.
NFPA 211 - 14.9
Flue Liner Integrity Zero tolerance for cracks/splits Fails if ANY tile is cracked
📖 Read the "Why"
Tiles fail due to thermal shock. Even a hairline crack breaks the seal, allowing heat and carbon monoxide to escape into the home's wood framing.

UL 1777
Liner Insulation 1/2" Foil-Faced Wrap OR Thermix
📖 Read the "Why"
Stainless steel conducts heat rapidly. Wrapping it stops heat transfer to the home's framing and keeps the inner liner hot, ensuring a fast, clean draft.
🏠 Clearances & Terminations
IRC R1003.9
The "3-2-10" Rule Min 3 ft above roof penetration Min 2 ft higher than structure within 10 ft
📖 View Diagram & Explanation
Measure 10 feet out. The chimney must be built at least 2 feet taller than anything in that circle to prevent wind downdrafts from pushing CO into the home.
